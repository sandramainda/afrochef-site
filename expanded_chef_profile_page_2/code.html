About Chef Amina

Chef Amina Diallo is a culinary artist from Dakar, Senegal, specializing in traditional Senegalese cuisine. With over 15 years of experience, she brings the rich flavors and vibrant culture of Senegal to every dish. Her passion for cooking began in her grandmother's kitchen, where she learned the secrets of authentic Senegalese recipes passed down through generations. Chef Amina's signature dishes include Thieboudienne (fish and rice), Yassa (chicken or fish in onion sauce), and Mafé (peanut stew), all prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. She is dedicated to sharing her culinary heritage and creating memorable dining experiences for her clients.

Cuisine Origins

Senegalese cuisine is known for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and diverse influences from West Africa, France, and North Africa. Key ingredients include rice, fish, chicken, vegetables, and a variety of spices such as cumin, coriander, and chili peppers. Dishes are often slow-cooked to allow the flavors to meld together, creating a harmonious and satisfying culinary experience. Senegalese meals are typically communal, reflecting the country's strong sense of community and hospitality.
