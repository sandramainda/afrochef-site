Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a chef or caterer? expand_more

You can browse chefs and caterers by location, cuisine, or service type. Use the search bar on the homepage or the 'Chefs' and 'Caterers' tabs to explore profiles.

How does the subscription work? expand_more

Our subscription gives you access to detailed profiles, contact information, and special offers from chefs and caterers. Payments for bookings are handled directly with the professionals, not through our platform.

How do I contact a chef or caterer? expand_more

Once subscribed, you can find contact details on the chef or caterer's profile page. You can reach out to them directly to discuss your needs and arrange bookings.
