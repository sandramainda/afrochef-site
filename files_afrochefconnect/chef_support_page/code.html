Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my chef profile?

To set up your chef profile, first sign up on our platform. Once registered, navigate to the 'My Profile' section. Here you can add your culinary experience, specialties, photos of your dishes, and set your availability. A complete and attractive profile increases your visibility to potential customers.

What are the benefits of a subscription?

Our subscription gives you access to premium features like enhanced profile visibility, detailed analytics on your profile views, and promotional tools to reach a wider audience. Subscribed chefs are featured more prominently on our platform, leading to more booking opportunities.

How can I track my profile analytics?

Subscribed chefs can access the 'Analytics' dashboard. This section provides insights into how many users have viewed your profile, the search terms they used to find you, and your overall engagement rate. Use this data to optimize your profile and offerings.
