My Story

Chef Amina Diallo is a culinary artist specializing in traditional Senegalese cuisine. With over 15 years of experience, she brings the rich flavors and vibrant culture of Senegal to every dish. Her passion for cooking began in her grandmother's kitchen, where she learned the secrets of authentic Senegalese recipes. Chef Amina's signature dishes include Thieboudienne (fish and rice) and Yassa (chicken or fish with onions and lemon), each prepared with the freshest local ingredients and a touch of her unique culinary flair.

Cuisine Origin

Senegalese cuisine is known for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and fresh ingredients. Influenced by French, North African, and Portuguese culinary traditions, it features a variety of dishes that are both hearty and flavorful. Common ingredients include rice, fish, meat, vegetables, and a variety of spices such as cumin, coriander, and chili.
